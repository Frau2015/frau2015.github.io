Who is GreenTara?

Green Tara is founded by Biba Tamang, She has over 4 years of massage experience. She began her career with prental massage with her Doula clients, and the sucess that came inspired her to go on doing massage full time through Zeel and Southe.
Through Zeel and Southe she became one of their top massues with over 20 five star positive ratings and more than 100 massage sessions. Biba would like to open her own massage company where she can control more of the massage experience. And where she can reflect aspects of her Boudist South Asian Cultural hertiage.
